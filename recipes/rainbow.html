Rainbow Roll

Description:

So many colors! The Rainbow Roll is a staple in so many sushi spots because of its popular inside and fish variety used on top!

Ingredients

  • Imitation crab
  • cucumber
  • Avocado
  • sushi rice
  • seaweed paper
  • seasame seeds
  • Bluefin Tuna
  • salmon
  • Shrimp
  • Yellowtail
  • Whitefish

Steps

  1. spread rice on seaweed paper
  2. sprinkle seasame seeds over rice
  3. flip over paper
  4. lay immination crab in the center of the paper
  5. apply 2 avocado slices along with crab
  6. ada small amount cucumber that goes across the paper as well
  7. carefull roll up seaweed paper together
  8. use makisu (bamboo rolling mat) to shape the roll evenly
  9. along the roll add salmon, BF tuna, Yellowtail and Whitefish, and shrimp over the top
  10. don't forget avocado!
  11. use saran wrap on the roll before cutting to ensure fish on top cut clean
  12. make a cut in the middle of the roll
  13. put cut pieces together and cut in 3rds to create 8 pieces
  14. done!

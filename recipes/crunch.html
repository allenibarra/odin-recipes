Crunch Roll

Description:

The Crunch Roll is one of the most popular cut rolls and for good reason! The 8 piece roll lives up to its name with texture being the emphasis of the roll along with sweetness that comes from the eel sauce commonly used

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Seaweed paper
  • Shrimp tempura
  • Asaparagus
  • Avocado
  • Tempura Crunch
  • Eel Sauce

Steps:

  1. Spread out sushi rice on seaweed paper
  2. flip over paper
  3. add 2 pieces of shrimp tempura in the center (tails sticking out) followed by asparagus and avocado
  4. carefully roll seaweed paper togerther
  5. use makisu (bamboo rolling mat) to shape the roll evenly
  6. make a cut in the middle of the roll
  7. put cut pieces together and cut in 3rds to create 8 pieces
  8. organize neatly on plate
  9. get tempura crunch and sprinkle generously over roll
  10. drizzle eel sauce over roll
  11. done!

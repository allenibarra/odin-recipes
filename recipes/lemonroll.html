Bluefin Lemon Roll

Description:

This roll just recently came out and its kicking off its legacy with a BANG! The emphasis on this roll is a savory truffle taste with a refreshing touch of lemon!

Ingredients

  • spicy albacore
  • seaweed paper
  • sushi rice
  • lemon
  • truffle sauce
  • Bluefin Tuna
  • cilantro

steps

  1. spread out sushi rice on seaweed paper
  2. flip over paper
  3. lay spicy albacore along the center of the seaweed end to end
  4. lightly add truffle sauce alogn the spicy alabacore
  5. carefully roll up seaweed paper
  6. use makisu (bamboo rolling mat) to shape the roll evenly
  7. lay bluefin tuna slices over the roll
  8. use saran wrap on the roll before cutting to ensure fish on top cut clean
  9. make a cut in the middle of the roll
  10. put cut pieces together and cut in 3rds to create 8 pieces
  11. thinly cut lemon and place triangle slices on each piece of the roll
  12. lastly, dress the entire roll with cilantro covering the entire top
  13. done!
